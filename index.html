sans the skeleton

Sans is a fictional character in the 2015 role-playing video game Undertale by Toby Fox.Sans is a skeleton wearing a blue jacket, black shorts with white stripes, and pink slippers.Species Skeleton, Weapon Bones, lasers, telekinesis, Family Papyrus (brother).

Sans is a relatively short monster, having a height similar to Frisk, Alphys, Asriel, Bete Noire, and Chara. He wears a blue hoodie with a white t-shirt and black shorts with a vertical white stripe on the sides.Sans appears as a short skeleton with a dimpled smile, large eye sockets, and perfectly aligned teeth. He wears an unzipped blue hoodie, a white t-shirt/sweater, black shorts with white stripes, and a pair of sneakers/slippers. He has white pupils that disappear when he is serious or angered. When Sans uses telekinesis, his left eye flashes light blue and yellow, the colors for patience and justice, and his right pupil disappears.Sans the Skeleton is a well-known character in the indie game UNDERTALE, Named after the well-known font 'Comic Sans' and also based of the word 'sans' which means humorous and funny. He loves jokes and ketchup and has a brother named Papyrus which is also named after the font 'papyrus' and the word papyrus it self.Appearance. Being a relatively short monster that resembles a skeleton. Sans wears a blue hoodie with a white t-shirt underneath and black shorts that are striped white. After Hate, however, Sans's iconic white shirt is now a cyan-like shirt with a turtleneck. Sans is almost always seen with a smile on his face.When either absorbing a Soul of Determination or being injected with it, both Sans' eyes will turn blue, showcasing a significant power boost.Almost always showing a smiley expression and a laid back tone, Sans is a character who’s known to make puns and to be extremely lazy as well.

In the Underground, Sans had acted especially lazy because he knew that any progress he’d made would be reset. Though, in Glitchtale, when Frisk breaks the reset button, he begins to realize he finally has a future and is not as lazy as he was in the past, though he still acts laid back most likely because it's grown on him. However, when Frisk was wanting to Reset after they promised not to, Sans became immediately infuriated at Frisk for lying and being selfish (as it seemed odd that when a problem occurred, the first thing they thought of was to Reset without thinking of the consequences for their friends) and would have killed them in retaliation if not for Asriel.
